PepsFeeder is a media outlet founded by a small group of journalists who believed the best stories don't always come from the biggest newsrooms.

We operate on a simple premise: that clear, honest writing about the things that shape modern life — health, technology, money, culture — is a public good, not a commodity.

## How We Work

Our contributors are independent journalists, subject-matter experts, and experienced writers spread across different countries and time zones. Some write under their own names. Others choose not to — and we respect that. What unites them is not a brand or a political line, but a shared commitment to accuracy, fairness, and relevance.

We do not chase algorithms. We do not manufacture outrage. We publish what we believe is worth your time — and we leave the rest.

## What We Cover

Wellness. Technology. Strategy. Three categories that, together, cover the ground of a modern life worth living well. We are not a general news wire. We are a focused publication with a point of view: that understanding the world around you is the first step toward navigating it with confidence.

## Our Name

PepsFeeder comes from the idea of a "feed" that energizes — a stream of content designed to inform without exhausting. In an age of information overload, we aim to be the signal, not the noise.
