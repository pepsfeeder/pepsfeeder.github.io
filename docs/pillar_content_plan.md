# PepsFeeder: Pillar Content Strategy (Authority Building)

To establish PepsFeeder as a high-value, authoritative publication, we will develop 5 core Pillar Pages (2,000–4,000 words each). These pages will serve as "Hubs" for all related content on the site.

---

## Pillar 1: The Cognitive Resilience Blueprint
**Title:** Navigating Mental Health and Focus in the AI Era
- **Core Problem:** Cognitive fatigue, algorithmic burnout, and the erosion of focus.
- **Target Reader Intent:** Readers seeking to maintain mental clarity and high-performance in a digital-first world.
- **Internal Linking Map:**
  - Supporting Articles: "AI and Your Brain," "The Science of Digital Detoxing," "Mindfulness for Tech Professionals."

### Outline:
- **H2: The Psychological Impact of Algorithmic Landscapes** (Understanding how algorithms affect our dopamine loops).
- **H2: AI as a Cognitive Partner vs. A Cognitive Depleter** (Deep dive into the 'extended mind' theory).
- **H3: The Neurobiology of Deep Work** (How to protect focus in a world of notifications).
- **H2: Building a Sustainable Digital Detox Strategy** (Practical, science-led approaches to disconnection).
- **H3: Ethical Engagement with Technology** (The role of personal agency in tech use).
- **H2: Practical Mindfulness for the AI-Driven Professional** (Actionable stress-reduction steps).
- **H2: FAQ: AI and Mental Well-being** (Semantic SEO targeting).
