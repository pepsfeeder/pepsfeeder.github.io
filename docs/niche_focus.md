# PepsFeeder: Niche Positioning and Taxonomy Restructuring Plan

## Niche Positioning Statement (Primary Niche: Adaptive Wellness & Modern Longevity)
PepsFeeder is a high-signal publication dedicated to helping Gen Z and Millennials navigate the rapid shifts of the modern era. We bridge the intersection of holistic wellness, ethical technology, and global insight. Unlike generic news aggregators, we provide deep-dive, science-backed analysis and actionable strategies for mental resilience, physical longevity, and digital well-being. Our focus is on providing original, high-value intelligence that empowers readers to thrive in an increasingly complex and automated world.

## Taxonomy Restructuring (Hugo Config)

### Proposed Categories (To be updated in config.toml)
1. **Wellness:** Focus on gut health, bio-hacking, longevity science, and sustainable nutrition.
2. **Digital:** Focus on mental health in the age of AI, digital detoxing, and the ethics of social media.
3. **Strategy:** Focus on the future of work, productivity hacks, and navigating economic/technological shifts.
4. **Insight:** Focus on deep-dive geopolitical and environmental analysis with a personal impact lens.

### Categories to Merge/Remove:
- **News:** Merge into **Insight** (only if expanded to 1,200+ words).
- **Lifestyle:** Merge into **Wellness** or **Digital**.
- **Entertainment:** Remove (Low-value viral content does not fit the new authority mission).
- **Gen Z:** Refactor as a target audience label/tag rather than a category.

## Content Pruning Strategy
- **Audit Rule 1:** Any post under 600 words that is a simple news summary must be marked for deletion or "Expansion Required."
- **Audit Rule 2:** All "wp-content" migrated images must be audited for copyright and quality.
- **Audit Rule 3:** Remove all "satirical debunking" or "viral mess" posts that lack deep analysis.
- **Audit Rule 4:** Prioritize original interviews and science-backed citations over derivative "top 10" lists.
