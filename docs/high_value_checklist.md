# PepsFeeder: High-Value Content Validation Checklist

Every article published must pass this checklist to ensure AdSense quality compliance and niche authority.

## 1. Depth & Originality
- [ ] **Minimum Word Count:** Is the article at least 1,200 words? (Pillar pages: 2,000–4,000 words).
- [ ] **Unique Perspective:** Does the article offer an original analysis, not just a summary of other news sites?
- [ ] **Structural Rigor:** Does it include at least four H2 subheadings and two H3 subheadings for logical progression?
- [ ] **"What This Means for You":** Does it have a dedicated section for practical implications and actionable steps?

## 2. Authority & Expertise
- [ ] **Expert Citations:** Does it cite at least two authoritative, non-competitive external sources (e.g., .gov, .edu, .org, or industry-leading publications)?
- [ ] **Science-Backed Insights:** Are health or wellness claims supported by peer-reviewed research or medical consensus?
- [ ] **Author Bio:** Is the author’s expertise clearly defined in their bio?
- [ ] **No Thin Content:** Does it avoid vague filler language (e.g., "In today's world," "Everyone knows")?

## 3. SEO & Technical Quality
- [ ] **Semantic Headings:** Are H1, H2, and H3 tags used correctly for hierarchical structure?
- [ ] **Internal Linking:** Does it link to at least two other related "PepsFeeder" articles?
- [ ] **External Linking:** Does it link to at least one authoritative external reference?
- [ ] **FAQ Section:** Does it include a 3-5 question FAQ for Google's featured snippets?
- [ ] **Meta Description:** Is there a unique, benefit-driven meta description (150-160 characters)?

## 4. Visual & UX Quality
- [ ] **Optimized Images:** Are all images properly named, include alt text, and have a clear copyright source?
- [ ] **Readability:** Are paragraphs short (2-4 sentences) for digital readability?
- [ ] **Bullet Points/Lists:** Are complex ideas broken down into lists for easier scanning?

## 5. AdSense Compliance
- [ ] **No Policy Violations:** Does the content avoid prohibited topics (e.g., medical misinformation, extreme violence, illegal acts)?
- [ ] **Editorial Disclosure:** Is there a clear editorial policy linked in the footer?
- [ ] **Transparency:** Is it clear who wrote the piece and when it was last updated?
