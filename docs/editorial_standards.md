# PepsFeeder: Editorial Standards & Content Policy

This document defines the rigorous editorial standards required for all content published on PepsFeeder. To maintain "High-Value" status and AdSense compliance, every contributor must strictly adhere to these guidelines.

## 1. Editorial Mission & Integrity
PepsFeeder is a platform for high-signal intelligence. We provide original analysis, not just information. Every article must offer a unique perspective, a deep-dive analysis, or a synthesis of expert insights that is not available on generic news aggregators.

## 2. Content Volume & Structure
- **Minimum Word Count:** All standard articles must be at least **1,200 words**.
- **Pillar Content:** High-authority pillar pages must be between **2,000 and 4,000 words**.
- **Structural Requirements:**
  - Mandatory H1 title.
  - At least four H2 subheadings.
  - At least two H3 subheadings.
  - One "Practical Implications" section (minimum 250 words).
  - One FAQ section (3-5 questions).

## 3. Source Quality & Citations
- **Expert-Led Content:** Every article must be grounded in research or expert opinion.
- **Mandatory Citations:** At least two (2) external links to authoritative, non-competitive sources are required for every article.
- **Authoritative Sources:** Preferred sources include:
  - Academic journals (.edu).
  - Government data (.gov).
  - Leading industry reports (.org).
  - Peer-reviewed medical/scientific studies.
- **Prohibited Sources:** Generic news summaries, low-authority blogs, and AI-generated text without human fact-checking.

## 4. Prohibited Content Types
To maintain AdSense quality standards, the following are strictly prohibited:
- **Thin Content:** Short, generic summaries under 600 words.
- **Derivative Content:** Rewritten news snippets without original analysis.
- **Medical Misinformation:** Unverified health claims or "miracle" cures.
- **Listicles:** Low-value "Top 10" lists without substantive analysis.
- **Sensationalism:** Clickbait titles that do not accurately reflect the content.

## 5. Author Transparency & Expertise
- **Author Bios:** Every article must include a detailed author bio (100+ words) that highlights their expertise, experience, and why they are qualified to write on the topic.
- **No Anonymous Posts:** All content must be attributed to a real person.
- **Update Logs:** For evergreen pillar content, a "Last Updated" date must be visible to ensure content remains current.

## 6. Internal Linking & SEO
- **The Pillar Strategy:** All supporting articles must link to at least one relevant "Pillar Page."
- **Reciprocal Linking:** Every new post must link back to at least two related internal articles.
- **SEO Discipline:** Every article must have a unique meta description (150-160 characters) and descriptive alt text for all images.

## 7. Content Review & Approval Process
1. **Research Phase:** Sources must be verified before writing begins.
2. **Drafting:** Must use the "Article Expansion Template."
3. **Internal Audit:** Every draft is checked against the "High-Value Content Validation Checklist."
4. **Final Approval:** Editor-in-chief verifies originality, source quality, and technical SEO compliance before publication.

---
*Failure to comply with these standards will result in the immediate removal of the content and a review of the author’s credentials.*
